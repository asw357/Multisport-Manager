General Rules

Multisport Manager is a fair-play game. Players must respect the competition and avoid cheating or exploiting bugs.

Gameplay Rules

  • 🏅 Each player represents a country and manages their national team.
  • 🎯 Training and scouting require Medal Points (MP).
  • 📊 All results are based on fair-play simulations.
  • 🚫 Cheating, exploiting, or match-fixing is prohibited.
