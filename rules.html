General Rules

Multisport Manager is an online multiplayer game where players manage a national sports team. The following rules apply:

  • Respect other players and follow fair play principles.
  • Each player is assigned a country at random upon registration.
  • Medal Points (MP) are used for training and scouting new athletes.
  • Inactive accounts (30+ days) may be removed.
  • Admins have the right to adjust MP values and competition schedules.
