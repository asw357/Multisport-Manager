About the Game

Multisport Manager is a strategic online multiplayer game where you take control of a national sports team. Compete in various editions, train your athletes, and manage your strategy to win medals!

Game Features

  • 🌍 Manage a national sports team.
  • 🏅 Compete in multiple sports and events.
  • 📊 Train and scout new athletes.
  • 🎯 Set tactics and strategies.
  • 🏆 Earn Medal Points and climb the rankings.
